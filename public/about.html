About

Reversi or Othelleo is a strategy board game for two players, played on an 8Ã—8 board. There are sixty-four identical game pieces called disks. which are light on one side and dark on the other. Players take turns placing disks on the board with their assigned color facing up.

The first Othello tournament was the Japan Othello Championship in 1973. Four years later, in 1977, the first World Othello Championship (WOC) was held. There were only five nations at the first WOC. In 2005 the World Othello Federation was founded in Reykjavik, Iceland. Twenty-two nations founded the World Othello Federation (WOF). By the 2016 annual meeting in Tokyo, the World Othello Federation had almost 70 member nations!

If you are serious about becoming an Reversi Ninja, visit the World Othello Federation to begin your journey.
